# Punakha Dzong Heritage

Discover the historic seat of Bhutan's government and the winter residence of the central monk body.

## Highlights

- The Temple of the Great Bliss
- Sacred relics collection
- Traditional architecture
- Festival calendar
- Photography guide

## Visiting Information

Plan your visit to this UNESCO-recognized architectural wonder at the confluence of two rivers.
