## Artistic Vision

The mural features majestic forest guardians and wildlife, representing Bhutan's rich biodiversity. It showcases the nation's dedication to protecting its natural heritage for future generations, with imagery that celebrates both the flora and fauna of Bhutan's forests.

## Cultural Significance

This artwork reflects Bhutan's constitutional mandate to maintain at least 60% forest coverage and its commitment to environmental stewardship. It honors the traditional Bhutanese respect for nature and the interconnectedness of all living things.

## Location

Positioned in Gelephu Core Town, this mural serves as a visual reminder of the importance of environmental conservation and Bhutan's role as a global leader in sustainable development.
