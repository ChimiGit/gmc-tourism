## Artistic Vision

The mural depicts the harmonious relationship between farmers, their fields, and the natural world. It showcases traditional agricultural practices that work in harmony with nature, creating a sustainable cycle of growth and renewal.

## Cultural Significance

This artwork honors Bhutan's agricultural heritage and the farmers who maintain traditional, organic farming methods. It reflects the nation's understanding that true wellbeing comes from living in harmony with nature.

## Location

Located in Gelephu Core Town, this mural highlights the importance of sustainable agriculture and the role it plays in Bhutan's vision of a mindful, environmentally conscious society.
