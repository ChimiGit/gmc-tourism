## Artistic Vision

The mural beautifully intertwines the iconic Druk (thunder dragon) with the royal crown, creating a visual narrative that speaks to both tradition and continuity. The dragon, representing strength and protection, is depicted in harmony with the crown, symbolizing the wisdom and guidance of the monarchy.

## Cultural Significance

This artwork serves as a reminder of Bhutan's unique identity as a constitutional monarchy, where the royal family continues to play a central role in the nation's cultural and spiritual life. It celebrates the deep connection between the people of Bhutan and their royal heritage.

## Location

Located in the heart of Gelephu Core Town, this mural invites visitors to reflect on the enduring values and traditions that define Bhutanese culture.
