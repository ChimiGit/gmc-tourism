## Artistic Vision

The mural features a mosaic of faces representing different regions, ages, and backgrounds within Bhutan. Each face tells a story, creating a collective portrait of a nation united in diversity yet bound by shared values and traditions.

## Cultural Significance

This artwork highlights the human element of Bhutan's culture, reminding us that behind every tradition, festival, and custom are the people who keep these practices alive. It celebrates the warmth, hospitality, and resilience of the Bhutanese people.

## Location

Located in Gelephu Core Town, this mural welcomes visitors with the smiling faces of Bhutan, creating an immediate connection between travelers and the local community.
