## Artistic Vision

The mural features a bridge as its central motif, with communities on either side coming together through art, culture, and shared values. It represents the role of public art in building social cohesion and understanding.

## Cultural Significance

This artwork reflects the collaborative spirit of the Mindfulness in Colour project, bringing together artists, community members, and organizations to create something meaningful for everyone. It celebrates art as a universal language that transcends boundaries.

## Location

Located in Gelephu Core Town, this mural serves as a gathering point and conversation starter, encouraging dialogue and connection among residents and visitors from diverse backgrounds.
