## Artistic Vision

The mural uses flowing lines and meditative imagery to represent the inner journey of self-discovery. It incorporates elements of Buddhist philosophy and meditation practices, creating a visual meditation space in the urban landscape.

## Cultural Significance

This artwork embodies Bhutan's commitment to Gross National Happiness and the importance of spiritual wellbeing. It serves as a public reminder of the value of introspection and mindfulness in daily life.

## Location

Nestled in Gelephu Core Town, this mural creates a moment of pause and reflection for both residents and visitors, encouraging mindfulness in the midst of urban life.
